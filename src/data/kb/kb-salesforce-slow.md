# Salesforce Performance Troubleshooting

## Regional outage check first
Before personal troubleshooting, check system status for Salesforce degraded/outage in employee's region.

## Personal troubleshooting (single user)
1. Hard refresh (Cmd+Shift+R) or try another browser (Chrome recommended).
2. Disable browser extensions, especially ad blockers.
3. Clear Salesforce cookies and site data.
4. Test on VPN vs off VPN to isolate network path.
5. Check Salesforce Trust status page for incidents.

## When teammates also affected
Likely platform or regional issue — reference system status ETA, no individual fix needed.

## Chicago office note
Historical pattern: US Midwest CDN issues often affect Chicago and New York together. Check system status before personal troubleshooting when multiple colleagues report slowness.
